about us

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn scuba diving or an experienced diver seeking new underwater adventures, Fuerteventura offers a range of diving opportunities. Our school Diving Atlantis provides a safe and enjoyable experience for divers of all levels.

scuba diving courses padi

Learn to dive safely and confidently!
PADI Open Water diver
No experience required

This is the most popular scuba diving course and is the entry point for beginners. It covers essential skills, knowledge, and safety procedures for scuba diving. The course includes elearning (theory online), confined water dive with a long set of exercises, 4 open water dives and license by PADI that is the most famous scuba diving federation of the world. On the course you will practice essential scuba skills under the guidance of our experienced instructor. After completing the course OWD, you'll receive a certification that allows you to dive to a maximum depth of 18 meters in any country of the world.


If you have not enough time to complete full course, you can enroll on PADI Scuba Diver that is approximately half of OWD and lasts 1 day only. Later on you can upgrade your license to Open Water Diver in any PADI dive center of the world. The license of SD allows you to dive down to 12 meters.


Experience the breathtaking beauty of the underwater world and become a certified diver!

PADI Rescue Diver course teaches you how to prevent and manage diving emergencies. padi divemaster

Divemaster is the first professional level in the PADI system of diver education. As a PADI Divemaster, you will acquired a high level of diving knowledge, skills, and experience, and you will be qualified to assist PADI instructors with dive courses and accompany certified divers on recreational dives. The Divemaster course involves knowledge development, practical application, and hands-on training. It covers topics such as dive theory, diving physics, physiology, equipment, environment, and dive planning. You will learn about dive leadership and how to supervise dive activities, assist with student divers, and conduct certain PADI programs. To complete the Divemaster course, you need to have 60 registered dives, pass the final exams, which include written assessments, practical application evaluations, and a rescue assessment. Become professional in the dive industry!


Extra costs: Divemaster materials (270 euros), PADI federation annual fee upon completing the course (about 125 euros).
